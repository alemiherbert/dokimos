Who We Are

Proven, by definition.

We are an indigenous Ugandan engineering consultancy built on a simple conviction: Africa's infrastructure problems deserve African-led solutions.

We work across three integrated practice areas — infrastructure, buildings and architecture, and project advisory and delivery — spanning water, transportation, and energy engineering through to architectural design and full project management.

01
Proven, Not Promised

Every design is stress-tested against real site data, real climate projections, and real budgets before it reaches a client's desk.

02
Local-First Engineering

We design for the materials, labor markets, and maintenance realities of the communities we serve — not the conditions of a foreign template.

03
Built for Climate Reality

Resilience isn't an add-on line item. Flood, drought, and heat projections are built into every water, road, and power design from day one.

04
Integrity in Every Line Item

Transparent costing, honest timelines, and engineering sign-off we're willing to put our name on.

What We Do

Three practices. One standard.

Infrastructure, buildings, and delivery aren't separate problems in the communities we serve — they're one connected practice. We design and deliver across all three, held to the same standard of proof.

Infrastructure Engineering

Water, transportation, and energy systems engineered for growing demand and unpredictable climate.

  • Water treatment & distribution networks
  • Irrigation & watershed management
  • Road, bridge & drainage design
  • Hydroelectric & solar power systems
  • Rural electrification & mini-grids
  • Climate-resilient infrastructure design

Buildings & Structures

Architectural and structural engineering for facilities that have to perform as hard as the infrastructure around them.

  • Architectural design & documentation
  • Structural analysis & design (concrete, steel, timber)
  • Foundation & geotechnical design
  • Facility & industrial building design
  • Structural assessment & retrofit
  • Building code & regulatory compliance

Advisory & Delivery

Planning and delivery discipline that gets a design from concept to a functioning system on the ground.

  • Urban & land use master planning
  • Feasibility studies & cost estimation
  • Procurement & contract administration
  • Construction supervision & quality assurance
  • Stakeholder & regulatory coordination
  • Scheduling & risk management

How We Work

From assessment to a system that sustains itself.

Infrastructure fails when it's designed for a site visit instead of a lifetime. Our process is built to prevent that.

01

Assess

Site, hydrology, soil, community need, and climate projections — gathered and verified before a single line is drawn.

02

Design

Engineering grounded in local materials, local labor capacity, and realistic long-term maintenance budgets.

03

Deliver

Rigorous technical oversight through procurement, construction, and commissioning.

04

Sustain

Monitoring plans, maintenance training, and capacity handover so the system outlives the contract.
