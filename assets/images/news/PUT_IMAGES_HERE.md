# News post images

`news/index.html`'s `.news-card` markup expects one file per post, roughly
**1280×800px (16:10)** or larger:

- placeholder-1.jpg
- placeholder-2.jpg
- placeholder-3.jpg
- placeholder-4.jpg

The whole `#news` section in `news/index.html` (titles, dates, tags,
excerpts, images) is placeholder content — replace it with real posts
whenever you have them. To add/remove a post, copy/remove a `.news-card`
block in `news/index.html` and drop in a matching image.

Until real photos are added, the site shows clearly-labeled placeholders in their place — nothing breaks.
